First try plugging in x also known as direct substitution 

if u get 0 over 0 that is undefined 

then try factoring 

then try plugging in x again 

Example 
$lim_{x->3}\frac{x^2-2x-3}{x-3}$

plugging in 3 gives us $\frac{0}{0}$

so we factor the top giving us 

$\frac{(x+1)(x-3)}{x-3} = (x+1)$

plugging in 3 again gives us 4 and that is our answer
