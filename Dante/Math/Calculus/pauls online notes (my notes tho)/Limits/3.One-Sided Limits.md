RIGHT HANDED LIMIT 

$lim_{x->a^+}f(x)=L$
provided we can make f(x) as close to L as we want for all x sufficiently close to a with x > a without letting x be a 

LEFT HANDED LIMIT 

$lim_{x->a^-}f(x)=L$
