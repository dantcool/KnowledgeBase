
Definition - we say that the limit of f(x) is L as x approaches a and write this as 

$lim_{x->a}f(x) = L$

provided we can make f(x) as close to L as we want for all x sufficiently close to a, FROM BOTH SIDES without letting x be a. 

Limits are not concerned with what is going on at x=a. Limits are only concerned with what is going around x=a.
