# Example 1
Evaluate the following limit 
$lim_{x->2}\frac{x^2+4x-12}{x^2-2x}$

Plugging in 2 into x just give us $0/0$

First step would be to look what we can factor out 
in this case our top and bottom can both factor giving us 

$lim_{x->2}\frac{(x-2)(x+6)}{x(x-2)}$

this case we got matching top and bottom so those can go too

$lim_{x->2}\frac{x+6}{x}$

finally we can plug in our x giving us 8/2 or 4
