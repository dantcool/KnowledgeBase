$(x-h)^2 +(y-k)^2 = r^2$ Standard Form 
$(x-h)^2+(y-k)^2=r^2$ Center radius form 

example difficult 
$x^2 + y^2 - 8x+ 10y + 37 = 0$

group terms together and leave space in between 
= $(x^2 -8x   ) + (y^2 + 10y   ) = -37$

complete the square take half of non squares and square them and add them to the other side as well

$(x^2-8x+4^2)(+y^2+10y+5^2) = -37 + 4^2 +5^2$

now factor expression 

$(x-4)^2+(y+5)^2=-37+16+25$
=$(x-4)^2+(y+5)^2=4$

now plug into standard form 

center(h,k) = (4,-5) radius r = 2

our answer center radius form 
$(x-4)^2+(y+5)^2=4$
